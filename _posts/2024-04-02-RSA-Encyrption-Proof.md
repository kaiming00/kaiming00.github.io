## RSA Algorithm

Select two large prime numbers $p$ and $q$ ($p\ne q$), and let $n=pq$, $\phi(n)=(p-1)(q-1)$. Select the positive integer $w$ such that $w$ is a coprime with $\phi(n)$.  Let $d$ be the modular inverse of $w$ modulo, i.e., $dw\equiv 1 (\mathrm{mod}\quad n)$.
